Do you know what is Rubik's cube?

Rubik's Cube is a cube puzzle.

By rotating the different layers and faces of the Rubik's Cube, rearrange the small cube pieces until all faces are the same color. Solving Rubik's Cube puzzles requires a certain amount of logic and skills, often requiring multiple attempts and reasoning.
